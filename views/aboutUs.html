About us

TutoPoint was started by two brothers, Ziz and Jason Cheng. Ziz is a freshman at UC Berkeley studying computer science while Jason is a freshman in high school in Seattle Washington. Jason was lucky to have an older brother guide him through the complexity of figuring out what best to do in high school to maximize his chances of getting into a good university, but both Ziz and Jason felt that this guidance should be available to more high school students.
