About Us

TutoPoint is a digital college consulting platform designed to make your life easier. We take a innovative and fresh style of college consulting to the table:

Our guides are all current college undergraduates at the nations top universities studying highly competitive majors. They understand the struggle because a couple years ago they were in your same exact position, and they know how to get you in because they got themselves in. Read more about our mission.

The sessions that take place through TutoPoint are 100% digital. Finding, booking, scheduling, and meeting with your guide is all done with the click of a few buttons, and now college consulting is accessible from the comfort of your home.

Dynamic payments allow you to only pay for time you use. Traditional college consultants sell expensive packages for 3-4 years at a time. At TutoPoint, you only pay the guide during your time speaking with them, starting with only $60/hour!

The most modern college consulting experience featuring video call, screen share, shared documents, and much, much more. You can now seamlessly integrate anything you need help with straight into our platform and show your guide.
