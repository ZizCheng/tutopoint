Our mission

TutoPointâ€™s mission is to bridge the gap between college and high school students. What actually happens inside the classrooms, the dorms, and the cafeteria of top American universities remains largely unknown to prospective students. That is why our guides are 100% current, real time college students. Sessions with our guides help open the once closed doors of prestigious American universities and allows clients to peak into what life is like as a college student. In fact, most of the sessions scheduled with our guides take place in between their classes!

Beyond college consulting and questions about applying to these prestigious universities, our sessions offer a warm, welcoming and completely stress-free environment to engage in conversation about all aspects of college life.
At TutoPoint, you will feel at home at your dream university before you even arrive.
