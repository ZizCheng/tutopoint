How it works

TutoPoint sessions are focused on being as stress free as possible. When you register, you can choose from our wide team of guides and pick any that you want to talk to. When you schedule a time, the guide has 24 hours to confirm your session. Once our guide confirms the session, you may cancel it or reschedule anytime until 24 hours before the session begins.
Since a big problem with traditional college consulting is paying for more than you need, here at TutoPoint, you pay as you go. You only schedule a start time, how long you talk for is all up to you and the guide from there. The first 5 minutes with any guide is free, so you can have some breathing room and make sure you like the college student. From there, every 15 minutes we will charge $15 from your balance to pay our guides providing this service.
Fill up your balance any time instantly and schedule right away, our guides await you!
